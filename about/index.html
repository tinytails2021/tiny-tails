How it all Started!

Tiny Tornado- the gentle giant!

Tiny entered our lives and hearts in 2007. She was tiny by no stretch of imagination. A Merle Great Dane who had no idea of her built or energy.
She had a personality of her own and a very friendly demeanor. She brought happiness and joy to us, everytime!

She, sadly, crossed over the Rainbow Bridge in 2018 and is sorely missed everyday. But we see our Tiny in every canine friend we meet.

Tiny Tails Dog Boarding is our way of remembering Tiny and reliving our times with her. Rest assured your furry pet will get the same tender love and care that Tiny got.
